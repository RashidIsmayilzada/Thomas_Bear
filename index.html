About

Using his art to resonate with the human experience, Thomas Bear takes you on an emotional journey, blending vulnerability with a fresh, folky pop sound.

After years of writing and producing across different genres for and with other artists, Thomas has shaped his own musical identity. His sound combines his unique raspy voice with elements of folk, indie, rock, and subtle electronic touches.

His songwriting is sensitive, melodic, and deeply personal, creating a sense of recognition and connection for the listener. Acoustic instruments like guitar, piano, and strings play a central role in his music. By recording them live, he captures the raw emotion and honesty of each moment, adding to the purity of his work.

Now, together with his band, Thomas Bear is preparing to release his music and bring his artist project to the stage.
